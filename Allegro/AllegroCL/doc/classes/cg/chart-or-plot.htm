chart-or-plot

The non-instantiable superclass of chart-widget and plot-widget. Properties defined on this class may be used with either a chart-widget or a plot-widget instance.

Here are the properties that are supplied by the chart-or-plot class:

  • body-background-color
  • body-border-color
  • body-border-width
  • body-gradient-blend
  • body-gradient-direction
  • body-gradient-filling
  • body-gradient-intensity
  • chart-legend
  • chart-object-id-equality-test
  • chart-objects
  • draw-focus
  • draw-legend
  • extend-data-to-middle-of-border
  • footnote
  • footnote-color
  • footnote-font
  • icon-gradient-blend
  • icon-gradient-direction
  • icon-gradient-filling
  • icon-gradient-intensity
  • margin-above-footnote
  • margin-above-legend
  • margin-below-subtitle
  • margin-below-title
  • margin-outer-bottom
  • margin-outer-left
  • margin-outer-right
  • margin-outer-top
  • outer-border-color
  • outer-border-width
  • subtitle
  • subtitle-color
  • subtitle-font
  • title (this one is common to other widgets)
  • title-color
  • title-font
  • value-types
  • warn-on-insufficient-data

See cg-chart-widget.htm for an introduction to the chart-widget, much of which also applies to the plot-widget.

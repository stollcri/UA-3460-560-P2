bar-chart-view

The class of a bar-chart-view. The unique properties of bar-chart-view are:

  • bar-border-colors
  • bar-colors
  • bar-gradient-blend
  • bar-gradient-direction
  • bar-gradient-filling
  • bar-gradient-intensity
  • bar-fill-textures
  • bar-width
  • bar-spacing
  • base-line-color
  • base-line-drawn
  • base-line-width
  • base-value

The bar-chart-view inherits additional properties from chart-view.

See chart-widget and also cg-chart-widget.htm.

line-dashing

Arguments: stream

Returns, or sets with setf, the line dashing of stream. Values can be

  • :solid
  • :dot
  • :dash
  • :dash-dot
  • :double-dot
  • :long-dash
  • :dash-double-dot

stream should be a cg-stream.

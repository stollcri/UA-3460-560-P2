Rules

  1. 14 players required to be on roster
  2. You get all of a team's quarterbacks and kickers (ex. CLE Ks or CLE QBs)
  3. Must start 1 QB, 2 RB, 3 WR, 1 K each week. Players must be called in by name before their game starts and full lineup must be in before 1pm on Sundays. Lineups not called in on time will be the same as the previous week.
  4. FREE AGENTS: The waiver wire will be open Tuesday and Wednesday until 9pm. If more than one team wants the same free agent, the team with the worst record/lowest point total will get the player. A team can only cut another team for 1 free agent per week (multiple players can be acquired, but you can only cut for one). Transactions can still be made after the wire closes and the first to call gets the player. No transactions can be made after the regular season ends (Week 14). Transactions have a $5 fee.
  5. TRADES: The trade deadline is the start of Week 11. Teams involved in trades must be in contention to make the playoffs. Trades can be made at any time before the deadline. Trades have no fees. One team cannot pay another to trade.
  6. Teams will be split into two divisions (picks 1-5 and picks 6-10). The top two teams from each division make the playoffs. Playoff teams will be seeded 1-4 based on their records and matched 1 vs. 4 and 2 vs. 3. The winners play for the championship.
  7. There will be 14 regular season games. Playoffs will be Week 15 and the SuperBowl will be Week 16.
  8. PAYOUTS: Division winners get $175. The other two playoff teams get $125. The teams that make it to the SuperBowl can agree to split the remaining money ($400 + all transaction fees) however they want OR the loser gets $75 and the winner gets the rest ($325 + all transaction fees).

Scoring

  • Passing

    • TD = 4 points
    • 250 yds = 4 points
    • 300 yds = 5 points
    • 1 point for every 25 yds over 300

  • Rushing/Receiving

    • TD(rushing/receiving/return) = 6 points
    • 70 yds = 2 points
    • 100 yds = 4 points
    • 1 point for every 20 yds over 100
    • 5 catches = 4 points
    • 10 catches = 6 points
    • 1 point for every catch over 10

  • Kicking

    • Extra point = 1 point
    • Field goal < 50 yds = 3 points
    • Field goal >= 50 yds = 4 points

  • 2-point Conversions

    • All players involved in 2-point conversions will receive 2 points (pass/rush/receive)
